Welcome, to the Digital Integration Teaching Initiative's (DITI) module on analyzing podcasts and TEDTalks. In this module, we will consider the structures, rhetorics, and strategies commonly used in podcasts and TEDTalks. We will use case studies and ask critical questions to analyze these two very popular modes of information delivery.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Understand TEDTalk and podcast anatomy and devices
  • Critically analyze structural and stylistic conventions
  • Identify areas of potential exploration, development, or change.
