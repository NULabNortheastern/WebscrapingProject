Just like TEDTalks Podcasts too have some important context to consider. Following are a few of them:

  • Due to the sheer volume of podcasts available, it can be very difficult for podcasts to gain an audience
  • The price of production for “professional-sounding” podcasts (technology, software, promotion, labor) can be prohibitively expensive without advertisements or corporate sponsorships 
  • Podcasts have to follow detailed protocols for copyright and fair use
  • Because podcasts are edited in post-production, interview materials can be edited, redacted, or otherwise misrepresented
