Ethical considerations

Because of all this, it is important to consider:

  • How are we being represented online? 
  • How are our data being used? 
  • Who is using our data and for what purposes?
  • How might our data be used in the future?

Watch this brief video (Links to an external site.) from The Guardian, of the author and broadcaster Timandra Harkness, and think about the following:

  • How are experts and researchers using big data in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic? Have you read any article that shared results from a big data analysis? What different types of data or dimensions did the article use? Was it really "big data" or was it just large data?
  • Are large-scale monitoring and surveillance systems a breach of individuals' privacy? In a pandemic, is the tradeoff between (some level of) privacy and the health/safety of a community/nation worth considering?
