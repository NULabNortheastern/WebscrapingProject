The Data in NVivo are all the research materials you are using (scholarly articles, transcriptions, newspapers, research notes, etc.).

Files will be where you can access all the research materials you have imported into this project.

  

Important reminder

NVivo can import many types of files, including .docx, .pdf, .doc, .csv, .png, .jpeg, .txt, video/audio files, and more. 

You should always save your original documents on your local computer or in cloud storage, even if these documents are imported into NVivo. NVivo can store documents, but it is more of an organization and analysis tool, rather than a storage option.
