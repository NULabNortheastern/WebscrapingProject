You can use NVivo to visualize your data and research in order to boost your arguments and prove your reasoning for data interpretations. These data visualization options help you explore your codes:

  • Chart: bar chart of the number of times codes were used in a file
  • Hierarchy Chart: visualizes number of codes used in a file
  • Explore Diagram: explore the codes used in a file
  • Comparison Diagram: compare the nodes used in multiple files

Here's where to find these visualization options on your system. 

1) Windows Explore tab

  

2) Mac Explore tab
