Alie Ward’s Ologies is popular with a variety of listeners because: 

  • The informal, conversational style creates a sense of rapport with listeners of all backgrounds
  • Humor, narrative asides, and audio clips keep listeners engaged by varying the structure of the conversation
  • The host creates a sense of community by directly engaging with audience questions and comments 
  • The host builds anticipation (even through “dry facts” and advertisements) with delayed punchlines and “sneak peeks”
