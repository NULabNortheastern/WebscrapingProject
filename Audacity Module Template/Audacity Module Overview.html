Summary of Module

Welcome to the Digital Integration Teaching Initiative module on podcasting with Audacity. This module will introduce students to podcasting and the basics of Audacity.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand narrative devices used in podcasting
  • Share techniques for writing, recording, and editing podcasts
  • Install and learn how to use Audacity as an audio-editing tool
  • Understand the steps for recording, editing, and sharing a podcast
