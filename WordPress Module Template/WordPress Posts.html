POSTS are blog or news posts that show up as you publish them, usually in a feed. 

From the “Posts” section on the side menu, you can add a new post or see all the posts on the site. Adding a new post will bring you to a blank blog post, where you can add a title and blocks of texts, images, words, links, etc.
