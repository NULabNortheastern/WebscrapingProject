The very first step in developing web content is to establish the concept for your website. Before you begin building the website, it is helpful to identify the siteâ€™s core purpose, and then brainstorm all your options and ideas. You can then develop a streamlined list of content that you would like to build and publish over time. Having a plan will make the website building process more efficient and the website more coherent. You can draw upon the examples listed on the previous page and use the following pointers to begin brainstorming.

Questions to consider when planning a website:

  • What is the purpose of this site?
  • What are the essential components I need to get this point across?
  • How can I make my site easy to use?
  • Who is my audience?
  • What site features will best support my goals and audience?

Components of a successful WordPress site

  • About and Contact pages
  • Responsive design (something that will draw in the audience and that looks good on multiple platforms)
  • Theme/format compatible with mobile phones
  • User-friendly navigation
  • Categories or tags (if you are writing posts)
