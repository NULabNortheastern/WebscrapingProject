WordPress site content can be of two types: Pages and Posts. We will first look at pages.

PAGES are the static pages that appear across your header menu. These will include any content that has a fixed location in your site's organization.

From the “Pages” section on the side menu of the Dashboard, you can add a new page or see all the pages on the site. When you add a new page, you can choose from a template, or select a blank page instead. When you add a new page, you can also select the type of page: About, Blog, Contacts, Menu, Portfolio, Services, Home, etc. There is a preview section on the right so you can see what the different template pages will look like.
