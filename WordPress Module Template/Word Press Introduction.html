WordPress is a professional website-building tool that allows you to build your own website, provides a free version of site hosting, and supports themes to customize your website. There are other website-building tools, such as Wix and Squarespace. WordPress is often used for blogging and academic websites; it has features for blogging, tagging, and categorizing text-focused posts.
