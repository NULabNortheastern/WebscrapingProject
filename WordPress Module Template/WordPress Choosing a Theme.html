The first way to customize is to choose a theme. A theme controls your WordPress site's appearance and functionality. 

In the left-hand column of your Dashboard,  open up the “Appearance” menu, and then click the  “Themes” button. The following image shows the 'Themes' page. (Reminder: if you need to get to the Dashboard from the "My Sites" page, click on "WP Admin" in the bottom left.)

Take a few minutes to go through the themes and see which might appeal to you. Depending on the content of your website, each theme will offer strengths and weaknesses; themes provide descriptions of how they can be used. You can play around with the different options and see what will work for your website (you can change your mind later too, but know that this can dramatically change the appearance and functionality of your website). There are plenty of very effective free themes for you to choose from. Remember the context and audience of this website, and choose a theme accordingly.

 

Note: If you have been invited as editors to the class website, then you should stay away from making changes to the whole website (e.g. changing the theme) and make edits to only your own pages.
