To get started with building a website using WordPress, follow these steps:

  1. Go to https://wordpress.com/ and click on "Sign up" or “Get Started” in the upper right corner
  2. Fill out the “Create a Site” information.
    • Keep in mind the purpose of your website while you are entering this information.
    • Choose a domain name that is professional. If you are building a personal website, then we recommend using your full name. WordPress provides a free domain with “.wordpress.com” at the end of the URL.
    • Use an email that you associate with your professional identity.
  3. Once you have created your site, you will be brought to a landing page for your site called "My Home." This page has some navigation links on the left, and a useful "Quick links" section on the right with options for editing your homepage, adding a new page, editing your menus, and more. This page is useful, but you will have even more options if you go to your Dashboard. To get there, scroll to the bottom of the page and hit the "WP Admin" link. 
  4. Your Dashboard will look like the following image.  The left side menu in the dashboard is the main way to navigate. You can use this menu to edit your posts, pages, and overall site design. It also contains a link to information about users, where you can update your user profile if needed.

Note: For some courses, you might have a class website. This means you will not create a new site or provide a domain name. In those cases, you will be invited as editors to the class website.
