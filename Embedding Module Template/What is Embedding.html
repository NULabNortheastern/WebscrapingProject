What is Embedding?

  • Integration of links, images, videos, gifs, and other content into blogs, news articles, and other web materials
  • Embedded content appears as part of a web page or slide and supplies a visual and/or audio element to increase engagement
  • Embedding relies on basic HTML, specifically iFrames
