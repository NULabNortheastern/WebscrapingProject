Embedding on a Wix Site

  • Embedding in Wix follows similar steps as on WordPress
  • To embed a StoryMap, use the iFrame code
  • To embed a YouTube video, you need the video URL
  • Wix embeds media by using different elements, such as “Video” and “HTML Embed”

Embedding a StoryMap on a Wix Site

1. Copy the iFrame code from the StoryMap site

2. In Wix, click “Add Section”

3. Choose the element “HTML Embed”  

 

4. You have options to choose where the embedded content will appear (such as top of the section, the right of the section etc.)

5. Click “HTML” in the upper left hand corner of the embed section  

6. Choose "HTML Code" under "Embed Type"

7. Paste the iFrame code into the section "Paste Your Code here (HTTPS Only)"

 

Embedding a YouTube Video in Wix

  1. Click “Add Section” on Wix
  2. Choose “Video” as your element
  3. Click the “Video” button in upper left hand corner of section
  4. Choose “YouTube/Vimeo” to embed a video using a URL
  5. Paste the URL in the section “Video URL”
