Embedding Best Practices

  • Remember to identify which HTML element (iFrames or <img> tags) is best suited for the task at hand 
    • iFrames for dynamic content, such as videos or interactive maps, <img> tag for static images

  • Keep in mind general best practices of design when embedding media
    • Don't use more than two complementary fonts
    • Make color choices that are accessible to everyone (and appropriate for the content)
    • Consider the readability when selecting images to embed into a website or StoryMap
    • Be consistent in your font and color choices!
