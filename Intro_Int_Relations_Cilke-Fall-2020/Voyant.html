Now that you've had a chance to think about data preparation and explore some tools for computational text analysis, it's time to try out a few additional tools on another platform: Voyant.

Voyant is a browser-based tool that lets you quickly upload a text, or set of texts, and begin exploring them. It provides a suite of many tools for counting words and ngrams, examining key terms in contexts, looking at patterns in usage over time, and many more possibilities. 

The handout that follows offers some quick notes on getting started with Voyant; after you've read through this, you can proceed to the slides for more step-by-step instructions and queries to try.
