  Zotero Metadata

In the information about an individual source column on Zotero (on the right hand side), there are various categories of metadata.

 

Info: Bibliographic Information (Title, Author, Year, etc.)

Notes: Your Notes (also sometimes notes are included when you enter ISBN numbers)

Tags: Keywords (also sometimes keywords are included when you use ISBN numbers)

Related: Other sources that may be related
