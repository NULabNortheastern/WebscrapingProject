Website Development

Website development refers to the processes by which you plan, design, and build websites on the internet.

Today, anyone can build their own personal and professional websites using free website building platforms like WordPress and Wix. Individuals no longer require proficiencies in coding languages to develop a website; however, having coding skills can certainly benefit you in customizing and designing your own unique webspace. 

This Module will Teach You How To:

  • Understand the strengths of WordPress and Wix as website builders
  • Avoid common mistakes in website design
  • Understand best practices in website design
  • Discuss the merits of a variety of Wix and WordPress website examples
  • Begin planning your website through the creation of a wireframe
