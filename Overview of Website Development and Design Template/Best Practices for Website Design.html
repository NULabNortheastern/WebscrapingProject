Website Development Best Practices

A good way to start thinking about website development (planning and design) is to think about what you like as an internet user. Take a moment to consider the questions below and jot down some notes of your own. 

  • What are some best practices you’ve discovered either building a website or interacting with a website as a user? 
  • What do you like most about the websites you frequent? 
  • What bothers you?

Here are some quick tips to jumpstart your thinking about website development: 

  • Always remember your audience and think about how you want to represent your identity and your work to that audience.
  • Use the “Preview” button (available on both WordPress and Wix) to see how your site will appear to your audience.
  • More ≠ better! You want your website to be easy to navigate, informative, and appealing.
  • Pick a theme/format that is compatible with mobile phones.
  • Design choices should be based on the content you are communicating.
