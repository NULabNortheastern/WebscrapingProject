Effective infographic projects

  • Contain appealing and vivid details (e.g. charts, visuals)
  • Have a specific and easy-to-understand title; concise and brief set-up 
  • Offer an effective demonstration of project's goal and provide information aimed toward the proposed audience
  • Make the problem relevant and appeal to the audience
  • Contain excellent syntax and vocabulary, explain terms/jargons, has proper citation
