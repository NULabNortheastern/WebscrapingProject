Welcome to the Digital Integration Teaching Initiative's (DITI) module on Infographics. In this module, we will introduce you to infographics as a mode of conveying information in a visually appealing way. We will also demonstrate how to make infographics using Canva. 

Learning Goals

  • Review elements, formatting, and best practices of infographics
  • Understand what an infographic is and what it is used for
  • Make an infographic using Canva
  • Discuss big takeways of data visualization
