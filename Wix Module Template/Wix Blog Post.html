If you used Wix ADI, your website might already have a “Blog Posts” page.

If not, go to “Add” > “Apps” > “Blog”, to add a blog page to your website. 

Go to your blog page: (“Pages” > “Blog” > "Post")

  

Hover over your blog post section and click “Manage”

  

The "Manage" option will take you to the blog dashboard where you can "Create New Post" or "Edit" an existing blog post.
