Wix gives you the option to let Wix Artificial Design Intelligence (ADI) create a website for you; you can also create a website using Wix Editor. We recommend that you use the ADI option, because it is super easy and fast! The Wix Editor provides users more control over the design but it requires more understanding of design and time. This module is primarily for beginners and focuses on using Wix ADI for designing websites.

Once you select Wix ADI for creating your website, It will ask questions about the purpose of your site and provide you with some options. Here are some example questions and options:

  • What type of website do you want to create?
  • What do you need on your new website?
  • Do you want to import images and text?  (you can skip this step)
  • Review and Edit your info
  • Pick a Theme
  • Pick a favorite homepage design

Go through the questions and provide responses or skip the steps (you can revisit or edit the pages and contents later), and at the end, ADI will generate your website.
