In your website, each page should have a particular purpose and content. Some of the commonly used pages in professional websites are Homepage, Blog page, and About page. You can add, edit and rename your pages by clicking the dropdown "Page" button on the top left corner. You can also use the 'Add' button to add pages.

   

The 'Design' button will help you change the design of your website, either the overall theme, or specifics like colors, fonts, and animation.
