Now it's your turn to create, edit, and play with your Wix site!

  • Edit a section on your homepage to include an area for media (a gallery of photos, a video, etc) 
  • Create a new page called “About Me.” 
    • Add a text section
    • Add an image section & an image
  • Create a new page called “Professional Experience” (or Resume, CV, etc)
    • Add a list section with your education
    • Add any previous working experience
  • Publish your site so you can share it with your colleagues!

 

As you explore website building, keep the following in mind:

  • Always remember your audience and think about how you want to represent your identity and your work to your audience.
  • For any questions you have, there are probably YouTube videos or written tutorials to provide an answer; just make sure you are looking at the correct Wix version.
