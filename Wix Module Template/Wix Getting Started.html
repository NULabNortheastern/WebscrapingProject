To get started, follow these steps:

    1. Go to https://www.wix.com/ and click “Sign In” in the upper right corner
    2. Sign up for a Wix account. We recommend creating a Wix account with your professional or academic email.

 

3. With your Wix account, you can make multiple websites. To create a new website, click “Create New Site”

4. Once you click "Create New Website", Wix will ask "What kind of website do you want to create ?" Here are our recommendations for the kind of website you can create for a class: 

      • Blog*
      • Portfolio & CV
      • Other

*If you choose blog, your homepage will be your blog posts. This can be changed later.
