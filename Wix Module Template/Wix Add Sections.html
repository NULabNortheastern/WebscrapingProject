You can add content to your pages using “Add: Section to Page.” Choose your content and drag and drop it to the area on your page where you want the content to appear.

Types of Sections to Add

  • Elements: The elements are your basic sections, such as texts, images, videos, photo galleries, and more.
  • Optional: Based on your website’s purpose according to your ADI’s results, you will get some recommended sections. For example, if you chose “Portfolio & CV,” your optional sections might revolve around your work experience.
  • More Sections: Here, you will find sections that have specific purposes, such as FAQs, Instagram, Subscribe, Testimonials, and more. 
  • Forms: These are forms that people can submit through your websites, such as a Testimonial Form or Job Application.
