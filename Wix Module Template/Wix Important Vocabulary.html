• Pages: These are the main areas of your website that your audience will be able to navigate on your site’s menu. You can add and delete pages on your Wix dashboard; when you add pages, these will show up automatically on your site’s menu.
  • Domain: Your domain name is the unique URL that leads to your website. Wix provides a free domain name that will look like “username.wixsite.com/website_name.” You can also purchase a domain name if you plan to use this website professionally or more frequently.
  • Section: Each page is divided into large chunks of content called “sections.” These sections (images, text, videos, etc.) can be easily swapped around to rearrange the layout of the page. 
  • Apps: These are specific Wix-created applications that you can add to your site, including blog posts and different types of forms.
  • Add: The “add” feature allows you to add new pages, sections, and/or applications
  • Blog: Wix provides a “Blog” app option, and this blog application will allow you to add, edit, and publish blog posts on your Wix page.
  • Design: the aesthetic choices for your website, including colors, font, and more.
