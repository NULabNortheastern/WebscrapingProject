The end of ADI's series of questions will lead you to your website's homepage. The following is an example of a CV website. At the top is the toolbar.

  

If you have more than one website, then the button "My Sites" under the "Site" dropdown button on the top left corner will lead you to the homepage of your account where all the different websites will be accessible. 

  

My Sites is also the account homepage which is visible to you when you log on to Wix.

Once a website is created, it is available on your account homepage.  When you click "Select and Edit Site" on the account homepage, it will take you to the website dashboard. Here you can select "Edit Site" under the "Site Actions" button, and Wix will take you to the Editor.
