Wix is a professional website-building tool that allows you to build your own website; provides a free domain name (with “wix” in it); and provides sections, apps, and themes to customize your website. There are also other website-building tools, such as WordPress and Squarespace. 

Wix prioritizes customizable design and allows site builders to get as creative as they want.
