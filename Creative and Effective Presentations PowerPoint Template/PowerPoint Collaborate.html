PowerPoint lets you share the presentation, work with a co-author simultaneously, comment on slides, and chat while working on the slides at the same time.

For collaborative work in PowerPoint, you have to save the file on Microsoft OneDrive. PowerPoint will ask you to “Save to Cloud”—name and save the file in your OneDrive folder.

  • Select the Share option on the ribbon. You can control the role of collaborators to view only or editing (Can edit/Allow editing is checked by default).
  • Enter the names or email addresses of the people you want to share the presentation with.
  • Select the drop-down to change permissions. Allow editing is checked by default. To change permission to view only, uncheck this box and select Apply.
  • Include a message if you'd like and select Send

 

 

 

After you share your file, you can do the following:

  • Work together at the same time (Co-Author)
    • Under Share, you can see who else is working on the file. 
    • Colored flags show where each person is editing. 
  • Comment on the presentation
    • Select Review > New Comment. Or select New if the Comments pane is open.
  • Chat while editing
    • If there's another person editing your file, select their image or initials to open a chat window.
    • Note: Your conversation won't be saved when you close a file.
