Welcome to the Digital Integration Teaching Initiative's (DITI) module on making creative and effective presentations. In this module, we will go over some strategies and best practices for digital communication and presentation. Specifically, this module will teach you how to use Microsoft PowerPoint software to make impactful presentation slides. This module will share tips, show examples, and provide step-by-step instructions for key functions for you to learn from as you develop your own presentations.

Learning Goal

Make creative and effective presentation slides to deliver a visually impactful and clearly communicated presentation.

Learning Objectives

  • Learn the best practices for making a PowerPoint presentation.
  • Learn advanced design and collaboration functions.
  • Understanding how to consider accessibility in designing presentations.
