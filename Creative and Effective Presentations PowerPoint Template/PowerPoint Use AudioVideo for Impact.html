Audio and video clips can be an impactful form of communication. PowerPoint provides the option of adding one or more forms of media in your presentation. However, use additional media only if relevant and remember that audio and video clips can also add complications when you are presenting.

Some technical details:

    • Audio and video are available at the top-right when you click “Insert” on the taskbar.
    • When you add audio, you can upload it from your computer or record your own audio within PowerPoint.
    • When you add a video, you can link to an online video or upload one saved to your computer.
    • If you are presenting on Zoom/Teams or other virtual meeting modes, make sure to share the computer’s audio with the attendees.
