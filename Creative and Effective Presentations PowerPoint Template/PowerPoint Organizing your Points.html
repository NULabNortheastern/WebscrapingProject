Treat your presentation like any other form of argument, explanation, or narrative. Be intentional, and organize your slides carefully considering how to order and structure them so that you can get your message across.

Be mindful of your audience, and don't make them work too hard. This means you should put your main ideas front-and-center, adding any additional detail in the “Notes” section. Think about the type of presentation you would like to see.

Use signposts to orient your audience. If your PowerPoint has different sections or chapters, use signpost slides to separate them. This will help your audience keep up with your argument.
