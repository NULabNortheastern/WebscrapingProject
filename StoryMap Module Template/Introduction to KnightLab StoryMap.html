Knight Lab StoryMap is a user-friendly, web-browser mapping tool that allows users to choose a map and place markers across the map. Each marker can present images, text, videos, and audio clips. The goal, then, is to tell a story with these markers and guide your audience through a journey.

StoryMaps are distinctive for their ability to combine narrative slides with each location on the map. Knight Lab encourages you to devise a "strong location narrative" so that the points on your map do not feel disconnected to the reader.
