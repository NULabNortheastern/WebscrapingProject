Demo Maps

These demonstration maps produced by the DITI team walk through some of the different options that are possible with StoryMap, including some of the more technically complex ways you can customize your maps. Each map walks you through the key aspects of the StoryMap tool and the process of creating a project.

  • Concert Venues in Boston:  (Links to an external site.)A demonstration map created for the Digital History of Ethnic Boston course that shows concert venues in Boston.
  • Concord Area Trails and Parks:  (Links to an external site.)A demonstration map created for the History of Boston course that shows trails and parks in Concord.
  • Commonwealth: An Offshore Wind Hub:  (Links to an external site.)A demonstration map created for the Energy Democracy course that shows wind energy infrastructures.
