Before you start creating a StoryMap, think carefully about the audience you are trying to reach with your map. This will impact your language, the order of your points, and the media that you choose. Also, think about the tone of the map. Here are some questions to jumpstart your thinking process as you plan your project. 

Note that this module will teach you how to create a project with the base maps included in KnightLab StoryMap. For more on uploading your own base image, see these tips from KnightLab. 

Questions to Consider While Curating Your Content

    • What story am I trying to tell? What is the main point(s) I want to convey?
    • What locations are most necessary to tell this story accurately and expediently? 
    • What images/media will best represent this location? How will the image enhance the story I want to tell? 
    • How can I best tell this story/give a tour of a location? How can I integrate a balance of text, images, and other media forms?
    • Who is my audience? What kinds of information does that audience need?
